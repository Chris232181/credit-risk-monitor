Scoring Methodology

A transparent, rules-based composite risk model.

Composite Risk Score

Each fund receives a 0–100 composite score computed from six weighted factors. Higher scores indicate greater risk. All inputs are sourced from SEC filings where available; analyst estimates are flagged.

Composite = (0.25 × Redemption) + (0.20 × Leverage) + (0.20 × Credit) + (0.15 × Liquidity) + (0.10 × Concentration) + (0.10 × Governance)
